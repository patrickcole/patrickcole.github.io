Hello, I'm Patrick

I’m a Front-End Engineer who is driven to learn, create and explore with the web. My love for technology, art and science provide inspiration and creativity to my endeavors.

My first computer was an Atari ST and some of my earliest and favorite memories are punching away on the keyboard while learning my first letters and words. I treasure the moments spent alone learning how this wonderful piece of circuitry and software came together. These childhood experiences helped me find my choice of career and I am an advocate for teaching coding to children to assist with problem solving and logic.

When I’m not at my computer coding, you will probably find me playing some of my favorite video games. Titles such as The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain and The Elder Scrolls: Oblivion.

Being a kid from the 1980’s, I always jam along and tap my feet to some good synthesized music and reminisce about when movies were actually on film and albums had two sides. I love a good John Hughes classic and will definitely quote Airplane! if someone says the word “surely”.
